KASSE-1

A 1U CubeSat designed to revolutionize STEM education through real orbital science and hands-on learning opportunities for the next generation of engineers and scientists.

About KASSE-1

Educational Focus

KASSE-1 is built from the ground up to inspire and educate students interested in STEM fields. This CubeSat provides hands-on experience with real satellite technology, allowing young minds to engage with cutting-edge aerospace engineering and space science.

Advanced Sensing

Equipped with a comprehensive suite of sensors, KASSE-1 monitors and tracks environmental conditions in Low Earth Orbit. From radiation levels to thermal dynamics, every piece of data contributes to scientific discovery and student learning.

Sensor Suite

KASSE-1 is equipped with state-of-the-art sensors designed to gather critical data about the orbital environment.

RADIATION MONITOR

Measures cosmic radiation and particle flux in LEO.

THERMAL SENSORS

Tracks temperature variations across the satellite.

MAGNETOMETER

Detects Earth's magnetic field variations.

OPTICAL CAMERA

High-resolution imaging system for Earth observations.

ATMOSPHERIC SENSOR

Analyzes upper atmosphere composition and density.

GPS/POSITIONING

Precise orbital tracking and positioning system.
